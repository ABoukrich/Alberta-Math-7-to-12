      About This Site

      Built for Alberta Learners

      A free, curriculum-aligned learning resource for students, teachers, and parents across Alberta.

      Our Purpose
      Why We Built This

      Alberta Math 7–12 was created to give students, teachers, and families a single, reliable place to access curriculum-aligned mathematics content. With the rollout of Alberta's New Curriculum, we wanted to make the learning outcomes and topics accessible, approachable, and easy to practise.

      Whether you're a student reviewing before a test, a teacher looking for resource ideas, or a parent supporting learning at home — this site is designed for you.

      🎒

      Students

      Practice questions, formula sheets, and topic breakdowns to help you succeed on tests and build confidence in math.

      👩‍🏫

      Teachers

      Curriculum maps, outcome breakdowns, and resource suggestions aligned to the Alberta New Curriculum by grade.

      👨‍👩‍👧

      Parents

      Understand what your child is learning at each grade level and find resources to help support their math journey.

      🇨🇦 Alberta New Mathematics Curriculum

      This website is aligned with the Alberta New Mathematics Curriculum introduced as part of Alberta Education's curriculum renewal initiative. The new curriculum emphasizes deep understanding, problem-solving, and mathematical reasoning across all grade levels from Kindergarten to Grade 12. All content on this site reflects the current program of studies for Grades 7 through 12.

      Senior High Pathways

      -1 Pathway: University-bound students taking advanced topics in calculus, trigonometry, and algebra (Math 10C → 20-1 → 30-1)
      -2 Pathway: Post-secondary focused on applied math and statistics (Math 10C → 20-2 → 30-2)
      -3 Pathway: Practical and trades-focused mathematics (Math 10-3 → 20-3 → 30-3)

      Curriculum Strands

      • Number — understanding number systems, operations, and properties
      • Patterns & Relations — algebra, functions, and relationships
      • Shape & Space — geometry, measurement, and transformations
      • Statistics & Probability — data analysis and probability
