    About This Site

    Built for Alberta Learners

    A free, curriculum-aligned learning resource for students, teachers, and parents across Alberta.

    Our Purpose
    Why We Built This

    Alberta Math 7–12 was created to give students, teachers, and families a single, reliable place to access curriculum-aligned mathematics content. With the rollout of Alberta’s New Curriculum, we wanted to make the learning outcomes and topics accessible, approachable, and easy to practise.

    Whether you’re a student reviewing before a test, a teacher looking for resource ideas, or a parent supporting learning at home — this site is designed for you.

    🎒

    Students

    Full lesson notes, practice questions, formula sheets, and unit exams to help you succeed.

    👩‍🏫

    Teachers

    Curriculum maps, outcome breakdowns, lesson frameworks, and exam templates aligned to the Alberta New Curriculum.

    👪

    Parents

    Understand what your child is learning at each grade level and find resources to support their math journey at home.

    Alberta New Mathematics Curriculum

    This website is aligned with the Alberta New Mathematics Curriculum. The curriculum emphasizes deep understanding, problem-solving, and mathematical reasoning. All content reflects the current program of studies for Grades 7 through 12.

    Senior High Pathways

    -1 Pathway: University-bound — advanced algebra, trigonometry, pre-calculus (10C → 20-1 → 30-1)
    -2 Pathway: Post-secondary applied math (10C → 20-2 → 30-2)
    -3 Pathway: Practical and trades mathematics (10-3 → 20-3 → 30-3)

    Curriculum Strands

    • Number — number systems, operations, and properties
    • Patterns & Relations — algebra, functions, and relationships
    • Shape & Space — geometry, measurement, and transformations
    • Statistics & Probability — data analysis and probability
